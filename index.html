ABOUT US

Just two dorks in lesbians with each other.

John and Gabby have been officially together since August 27, 2017. They have one of the most interesting and complex love stories in all of history's existence. Time and time again, they face off against all obstacles but finish standing tall together. Gabby is a beautiful young woman who devotes most of her time to helping younger students pursue higher education. She has the two cutest dogs in the world, and loves to sleep in her free time. John is an adventurous spirit pursuing his interest in software development. He is hardheaded but goodwilled - he will challenge you in Super Smash Bros and most likely win. The two love going on adventures, but will probably choose staying in with each other than going out.

John and Gabby are to each other the way Kanye is to Kim. There is no doubt that they are good for each other, seeing how happy they make each other (they will also become really rich and famous one day probably). They are both blessed with loving and caring friends and family, and have already met each other's families. They first met each other through mutual friends and their mutual interest in Korean-American rapper Dumbfoundead (thank you Korean Jesus). They soon found out that they had a lot in common and were interested in both their similarities and differences. They hope to continue living their lives to the fullest and with each other.
